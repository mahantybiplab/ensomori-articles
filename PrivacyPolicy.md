# Privacy Policy for EnsoMori

**Effective Date:** June 2026

## 1. Introduction
EnsoMori respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the EnsoMori application.

## 2. Data Collection and Storage
EnsoMori is designed with a strict "local-first" philosophy:
* **Local Storage:** All of your journal entries, intentions, moments, and voice recordings are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit, back up, or store this data on external servers.
* **No Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics trackers, advertising SDKs, or background tracking software.
* **No Account Required:** You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personally identifiable information to use EnsoMori.

## 3. Data Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to outside parties. 

**Voluntary Feedback:**
The only time data leaves your device is if you voluntarily choose to use the "Share Feedback" feature. When you use this feature, your email client will open with a pre-filled draft. This draft includes your feedback message, basic system information to help us troubleshoot bugs (such as your device manufacturer, device model, Android OS version, the app version, and your device's basic timezone setting to debug daily streak issues—this does NOT use GPS location), and anonymous usage statistics (such as your total number of check-ins, total number of journal entries, and days present). It will **never** include the actual content of your journal entries, intentions, moments, or audio recordings. Because it opens in your own email app, you have full visibility and control to edit or delete any of this information before pressing send.

## 4. Audio Recordings (Microphone Access)
EnsoMori requests microphone access solely for you to record personal "AfterMath" audio messages for yourself. These audio files are saved securely in your device's internal, protected storage and are never uploaded or shared.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
